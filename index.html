How to play

  1. Select a stat from your card.
  2. HP and Speed compare directly with your opponent.
  3. Attack and Special Attack battle against the opponent's defensive stats.
  4. Defense and Special Defense confront the opponent's offensive stats.
  5. Type multipliers apply to offensive and defensive stats.
